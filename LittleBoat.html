Here's a brief summary of the history of boatkind

Boats used to be pretty small and cute back in the old days. But then the human population started to grow and with it, the need for transportation due to trade and travel. So the brilliant idea to create bigger boats came about. That's how many of the most famous boats were invented, such as the Titanic. From there, boats just kept getting bigger and longer. Sure small boats are still around but they're mostly used for private trips or fishing as a hobby. The larger ones are used for businesses such as the fish market or cruises.
